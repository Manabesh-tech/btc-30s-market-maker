Factor Explainer

Top-5 book imbalance

Main directional signal. Positive means bid-side depth is heavier than ask-side depth near the top of book.

Signed persist 0.5 / 0.6

How long imbalance has stayed beyond those thresholds. Positive means persistent bid pressure, negative means persistent ask pressure.

Microprice deviation

Top-of-book lean versus simple mid. Helps detect whether the best bid/ask pressure is already tilted.

Short vol

Used as context, not the main directional driver. High short-term noise should shrink confidence.

Thin depth

Flags when current top-10 depth is shallow relative to recent normal, so dramatic imbalance is treated more cautiously.

Public stream PnL

Best payout wins the trade. Settlement uses platform entry and settled prices from the public trade feed.
