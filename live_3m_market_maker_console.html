Implementation view

Signal: last `3m` move in bps from live Binance mid-price history.
Dangerous side: mean-reversion side. After an up move, `Lower` is dangerous. After a down move, `Higher` is dangerous.
Probability: either a conservative phase-1 schedule or the raw backtest schedule.
Quote engine: constant-edge payouts from your entered edge, then clamped to the platform product range.
Current product limits from the public config are reused here: `65%` payout floor and `105%` cap for the `3m` product family.
